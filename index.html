Smart Nurse

A smart service by web application.

An AI-based project which uses denoising autoencoder, BART, to carry out abstractive text summarization on the dataset of COVID-19, using ROUGE scores as an evaluation metric. We achieved decent results and evaluated it on the ROUGE metric, used cross entropy loss as the loss function and "Ranger” for optimizer. The primary focus of the study has been the use of medical articles based on COVID-19, which is aimed to provide a significant support for the research purpose during the Coronavirus pandemic.
